What is DetectSus?

DetectSus is a real-time malpractice detection system that leverages advanced computer vision and AI to identify suspicious activities in classrooms. It helps safeguard exam integrity by monitoring unauthorized materials and unusual behaviors.

  • Object detection for identifying phones, notes, and more
  • Pose estimation to capture suspicious body language
  • Immediate alerts for rapid intervention
